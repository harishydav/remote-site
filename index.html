About us

We are an upskilling community school that prepares you for remote jobs.

Landing a good remote job can be difficult

We screen you, and train you, in remote working skills

By the very nature of remote positions, companies are looking for a totally different kind of soft skillset. This is alongside a very high benchmark of technical skill & cultural fit for distributed teams. There is a knowledge gap, within these skill sets, that leave most candidates handicapped for the remote work market.
